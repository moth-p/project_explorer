About the tour

  • Total Duration: 8 hours (including round-trip transportation)
  • Hiking Time: Approximately 4 hours
  • Daily Start Time: 8:00 AM
  • Location: The main dock in Ísafjörður town center
  • Meeting Time: 15 minutes before the tour starts
  1. Wear waterproof clothing and sturdy hiking boots with good traction.
  2. Bring plenty of water and snacks.
  3. Note that there is no mobile signal during the tour; inform family or friends of your plans beforehand.
  4. If you have any special dietary requirements, please specify them during booking.
  1. Free Cancellation: Full refund for cancellations made 72 hours before the tour.
  2. Partial Refund: 50% refund for cancellations made 24–72 hours before the tour.
  3. No Refund: No refunds for cancellations made within 24 hours of the tour start.
  1. This tour is not recommended for children under 12.
  2. Maximum Group Size:The tour is limited to 8 participants to ensure an intimate and personalized experience.
  3. Not suitable for pregnant individuals or those with cardiovascular conditions.
  4. All participants are required to sign a liability waiver before joining the tour.
  5. The schedule may be subject to change or cancellation due to weather conditions. If the tour is canceled due to severe weather, we will offer a full refund or an alternative date.
